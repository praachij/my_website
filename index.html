My Story

Built across borders.
Driven by impact.

My journey has taken me across four countries, from India to Japan, Singapore, and now the United States. I studied Economics at Nagoya University and NYU, then earned my Master's in Technopreneurship & Innovation from NTU Singapore. I'm a professionally certified Global Career Counsellor, and I've spent years building, advising, and mentoring.

Beyond careers, I founded GEET, a non-profit dedicated to girls' empowerment through education. My work in impact and leadership earned me the McKinsey Next Generation Women Leaders Award. I've been a World Bank Youth Summit delegate, organized education fairs, and built communities that help young people dream bigger.

This isn't theory for me. It's lived experience, and I bring all of it to every coaching conversation.
